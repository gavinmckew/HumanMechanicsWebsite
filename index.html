INTRODUCTION

Dan Anderton

Dan Anderton is the director of DAHM who strives for honest, cost effective and appropriate treatments and advice for his clients. With a background in applied sports and exercise science blended with sports medicine and sports therapy to a Masters degree level, he holds a wealth of knowledge and continues to share this during client appointments, workshops and within the academy. With many years experience working with the general population to travelling the world with elite sporting athletes, Dan has gained vast experience related to general aches and pains to specific pathologies and post surgery work.

The DAHM ethos centres around client education and building a more robust and functional person, whether you are an elite level athlete or not even an exercise fan. The team have the tools to help unlock more efficient movement patterns, help to reduce painful symptoms and strive to reduce the risk of future injury.
